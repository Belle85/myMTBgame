Instructions

You will be given a random performance number at the start of the race.

There are four different parts for your bike below.

By clicking on one part you will add a specific amount of performance points to your total score.

You win the race by reaching excatly your performance score, you loose the race if your total performance score goes above requirement. Don't over do it!

The performance improvement value of each part is not provided until you click on each piece of equipment and the value changes everytime a new race start.
